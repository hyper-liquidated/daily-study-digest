Signal and Structure

The Paradox of Choice Revisited
Journal of Experimental Psychology: General, 2024
Summary: Revisits the famous 'jam experiment' and finds that fewer choices consistently result in faster decisions and greater satisfaction.
Why it’s notable: Strong real-world implications for marketing, UI design, and productivity systems.
The Inheritance Lottery: How Family Wealth Shapes Risk-Taking and Innovation
Quarterly Journal of Economics, 2024
Summary: Shows unexpected inheritances increase entrepreneurship — but only among top-wealth households.
Why it’s notable: Fresh look at wealth inequality’s influence on ambition and innovation.

Architectures of Capital

Rollups & Data Availability: Why Scaling Isn’t Just About Throughput
Ethereum Foundation, Celestia, StarkWare blogs
Summary: Rollups batch transactions, but without guaranteed data availability, they risk censorship and broken trust.
Why it matters: This underpins Ethereum's future. Data availability is crucial for modular security and scaling.
Applications: zkSync, Celestia, EigenDA
Tools: Proto-Danksharding, RollCall
Latency Arbitrage and the Hidden Economics of Speed
Budish, Cramton & Shim (2015 + 2023 follow-ups)
Summary: High-frequency traders exploit milliseconds for profit — at the cost of efficiency.
Why it matters: Reinforces need for exchange reforms like IEX, MEV protection in DeFi.
Applications: Fair market design, batch auctions
Tools: CowSwap, Flashbots, Jito
