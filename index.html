What Is a Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) and Why Should You Care?
(Originally from Batch 11, #2) Basic idea:

A ZKP lets you prove that you know something ‚Äî say, the password to a wallet, or that a transaction was valid ‚Äî without revealing the details of what you know.

Imagine proving you‚Äôre over 18 without showing your birthday. A ZKP can do that ‚Äî it proves the logic of the claim (age > 18) without exposing personal info.
